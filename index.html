Today Car Manufacturers integrates Smartphones / Tablets and App Stores into cars onto their instrumetal panel. They combine:

  • car signals,
  • phone techniques and
  • multitainment systems.

But these Panels have a lot of limitations and focused to the driver only.

The new Area: "Bring your own device" comes today to integrate Smartphones / Tablets and Apps within cars. Co-Driver and Fellow Passanger want to interact with the car too.

Since 1996 / 2001, cars sold in the U.S. / in the EU, have been required to provide a connection for mechanics and inspectors to measure what's going on electronically under the hood. The system, called On-Board Diagnostics generation two (OBD-II).

By bringing CAN to Ethernet Gateways and combine Ethernet, Bluetooth and HTML5 Websocket Technique together you can boost customer's perception.

The Automotive Dashboard SDK and Hardware KIT gives you a Toolkit to create costom dash boards and instrument screen prototypes.

It's good for rapid development and use fast data update rates.

You can also plugin your own sensors (CAN-Bus or Ethernet) or combine sensors from your Smartphone / Tablet (GPS, Acceloration ...). Use your own video-cams for front or backview of the street or have a look into your engine bay.
